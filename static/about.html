About SNet

SNet is a private social networking platform exclusively for Salesian College students. Designed as a space for meaningful discussions, connections, and campus updates, SNet brings the college community together in a simple and distraction-free environment.

Who We Are

We are Pralad, Subhankar, and Nisha, three caffeine-fueled BCA students who decided to build something cool instead of just another boring assignment. Armed with late-night coding sessions, countless bugs, and an unhealthy amount of snacks, we crafted SNet as our final-year projectâ€”a platform we hope will bring students together long after we graduate.

Vision

One of the biggest challenges students faced was the cumbersome process of sharing college event updates across multiple WhatsApp groups for different departments. Important information often got buried in endless chats. SNet solves this by providing a central hub for all college-related discussions and announcements.
